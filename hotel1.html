Ganesh Dham

The room is named after a natural Dham of Sokpey village. A natural lord Ganesh statue was discovered by locals especially cattle rarers who keep on moving for green pastures. After it was discovered the local people gathered and payed their obeisance to Lord Ganesh in the form of Yagnas (Holy fires). It is believed by the locals that after the discovery of the Statue the landslide of the village decreased considerably, they consider it as a blessing from the Lord.

KIRATESHWAR DHAM

This room is named after a holy Dham called Kirateshwar Dham located 25kms away from the hotel towards the west direction. This is actually a Shiva temple where lord Shiva had taken the form of Kirateshwar during the spiritual tour to Van Vicharan. This spot is located in South Sikkim on the banks of the river Rangit on the way to Gyalshing-Pelling. The Kirateshwar temple geographically lies in Hingdam village of South Sikkim which touches the border of West district.
